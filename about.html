About Me

I'm a dedicated software developer and technical writer passionate about delivering value. My primary expertise lies in React, Next JS, JavaScript, CSS, and HTML.


I specialize in writing comprehensive articles and building websites with these technologies. Additionally, I am proficient in Node JS, Express, Python, MongoDB, and MySQL. My ability to learn and write about new technologies swiftly highlights my resilience and adaptability.


I currently work at freeCodeCamp as an instructional designer. I also work on freelance projects with some incredible people I knew from Twitter. Other times, I work on personal projects I don't ever complete.


I am committed to producing reliable and high-quality content. You can read my articles on freeCodeCamp and my blog. You can learn about what I do on my service page.

freeCodeCamp is a donor-supported tax-exempt 501(c)(3) charitable organization (United States Federal Tax Identification Number: 82-0779546).

Our mission: to help people learn to code for free. We accomplish this by creating thousands of videos, articles, and interactive coding lessons - all freely available to the public.

Donations to freeCodeCamp go toward our education initiatives, and help pay for servers, services, and staff.
